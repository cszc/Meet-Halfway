About

Find a convenient place to meet, safely and securely. Meet Halfway makes it easy to find a place to meet a friend or stranger that's convenient for both of you.


Enter your starting point, transit mode, and type of place you want to meet


Send a link to your friend for them to separately enter their address and transit mode


Discover destinations with roughly equal travel times for both of you

Meet Halfway was created by CAPP graduate students Christine Chung, Lauren Dyson, Paul Mack, and Leith McIndewar for CS122 at the University of Chicago. Feel free to drop us a line.
